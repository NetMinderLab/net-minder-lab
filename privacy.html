Information We Collect

Net Minder Lab may store goalie names, team names, seasons, games, shot records, save and goal results, clears, endline wins, report-card notes, achievements, and related goalie performance data that you enter into the app.

If you add profile photos or team logos, the app uses the photos you choose from your photo library for those in-app profiles. Net Minder Lab does not scan your photo library or access photos you do not select.

If you contact support, we may receive your email address and any information you include in your message.

Sign in with Apple

If you use account features, Net Minder Lab uses Sign in with Apple to identify your account. Apple may provide a name or relay email depending on the choices you make during sign-in.

iCloud Sync and Sharing

If you use iCloud sync or family sharing, app data is stored in Apple iCloud using CloudKit so it can sync across your devices or be shared with people you invite.

Purchases

If you purchase or restore a subscription, the transaction is handled by Apple through the App Store. Net Minder Lab checks App Store purchase status to unlock premium features.

How We Use Information

We use app data to provide live scoring, goalie dashboards, shot charts, report cards, progress insights, PDF sharing, sync, family sharing, subscription access, support, and app improvement.

We do not sell your personal information. We do not use your goalie performance data for third-party advertising.

Data Storage

Data may be stored locally on your device. If you enable iCloud features, data may also be stored in your iCloud account through Apple CloudKit.

Sharing

You control report sharing and iCloud family sharing actions from inside the app. Data is shared only when you choose to share it or invite another person.

Your Choices

You can delete app data from the app, remove the app from your device, manage iCloud data through your Apple account, and contact support with privacy questions.

Children’s Privacy

Net Minder Lab is intended for use by parents, guardians, coaches, and athletes with appropriate permission. If you believe information was provided without appropriate permission, contact support so we can help.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy as the app changes. The updated version will be posted on this page with a new effective date.

Contact

For privacy questions or support, email netminderlab@gmail.com.
